My Projects

I challenged myself to recreate the Google homepage — yes, the Google! It was a surprisingly tricky task because, even though it looks simple, every pixel and spacing matters. Aligning elements, centering the search box, and making it responsive tested my attention to detail. It gave me a whole new appreciation for minimalist design — and now I can proudly say I (sort of) built Google!

I decided to recreate the Wildberries homepage to practice layout and design. It was challenging to replicate the product cards, filters, and overall structure. But it was a fun experience learning how e-commerce websites organize so much content in such a user-friendly way. While I didn't quite make it a shopping empire, I can proudly say I built my own version of Wildberries for practice!

So, I had the amazing opportunity to work alongside Pavel Durov on the creation of Telegram. Well... sort of! I built my own simplified version of the app to practice mobile app layout and messaging UI design. Replicating the chat bubbles, the menu, and the slick interface was surprisingly tricky — but also a lot of fun! While I didn't quite change the world with my version of Telegram, I can proudly say I collaborated with Pavel on this one!
